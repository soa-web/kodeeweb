FAQ

What does "don't love it, don't pay" actually mean? +

Exactly what it sounds like. We send you the finished page within 48 hours. You open it. If you don't want to use it, you reply "no thanks." That's the end. No invoice, no fee, no negotiation. If you do want to use it, we send a $497 invoice and hand over the files.

What if I want changes? +

One round of changes is included in the $497. Most clients ask for tweaks to copy or colours — totally fine. Bigger changes (e.g. completely different direction) usually mean we missed something in the brief, and we'll rebuild from scratch at no extra cost. Once.

Will I be able to edit it myself afterwards? +

Yes. We build on platforms you can update yourself — Wix, Framer, or static HTML depending on what fits. We send the login. You own it from day one.

Do I need to have a domain already? +

No. As a free bonus, we'll advise you on what to register and where so you don't get gouged. If you already have one, we'll point it at your new page.

Why is it so cheap? What's the catch? +

No catch. We're fast because we use AI to compress what used to be 3 weeks of agency work into 2 days. We're cheap because we don't have an office, account managers, or a sales team. You're paying for the build, not the overhead.

What's a one-page landing site, exactly? +

One scrolling page that does the job of a five-page brochure site. Hero, what you do, why you, contact form, WhatsApp button, footer. It's all most small businesses need. If you need a full multi-page site, mention it in the form and we'll quote separately.

Why only 3 slots a week? +

Because each build is real work, not a template. We cap it so quality holds. When this week's slots are gone, you'll wait for next week's batch.
